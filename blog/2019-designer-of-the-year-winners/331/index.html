2019 Designer of the Year winners!

By CSSDA crew 10 Mar 2020 update

Incredible talent, amazing work! It's time to reveal the most awarded agencies, studios and solo designer/developers of 2019!

The big news is that Red Collar (Best Agency) and Daniel Spatzek (Best Solo) have both won the top award twice. No easy feat! These dedicated and inspiring winners are living proof that hard work, consistency, creativity and a meticulous eye for detail are the foundation stones for award winning work. In addition to this we welcome Advanced Team (Studio) to the podium for their first DOTY win.

Best Agency of the Year: Red Collar

 

Best Studio of the Year: Advanced Team

 

Best Solo of the Year: Daniel Spatzek
