Website of the Year 2019 winners!

By CSSDA crew 5 Mar 2020 update

For the first time in WOTY history the CSSDA judges have given the highest score to a solo portfolio site! Congratulations to Bruno Simon on taking out Website of the Year 2019!

Producing work that surpasses the work of studios and agencies is a fantastic result and very well deserved. Within seconds of hitting the site it's easy to see why Bruno Simon's Portfolio has blown the minds of the judges. Bruno's site is brilliantly executed, wickedly interactive and highly original, from the animation to the color choices. This is one exceptional site!

On behalf of the CSS Design Awards community we thank all other contestants, judges and fans for another exceptional year of web design and development.
