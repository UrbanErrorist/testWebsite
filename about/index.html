TO THE POINT

I am a London-based freelance digital and brand designer with over ten years' industry experience.  I have worked both independently and in fast-paced digital agencies on a wide variety of projects for local and global clients.

I specialise in branding, web and user experience design. I believe in crafting bold, original and intuitive products. My core values are simplicity in design, attention to detail, and an understanding for my clients' needs. I am available for freelance, contract or direct client projects.
